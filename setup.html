SETUP GUIDE

How to Set Up NakedTurtle IPTV

Follow our step-by-step instructions to set up NakedTurtle IPTV on your preferred device. Our guides cover all popular platforms and are designed to get you streaming in minutes.

Amazon Firestick Setup Guide

1

Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

First, you need to allow installation of apps from unknown sources:

  • Go to Settings on your Firestick
  • Select My Fire TV or Device
  • Choose Developer Options
  • Turn on Apps from Unknown Sources
2

Install Downloader App

Next, install the Downloader app to download the IPTV player:

  • Go to the Search icon on your Firestick home screen
  • Type Downloader and select it from the results
  • Click Download or Get to install
3

Download and Install IPTV Player

Now, download and install an IPTV player app (we recommend TiviMate or IPTV Smarters):

  • Open the Downloader app
  • Enter one of these URLs in the address bar:
    • For TiviMate: https://tinyurl.com/tivimate-latest
    • For IPTV Smarters: https://tinyurl.com/smarters-latest
  • Click Go to download the APK file
  • Select Install when prompted
  • Click Done after installation
4

Configure IPTV Player with Your Subscription

Finally, set up the IPTV player with your NakedTurtle subscription:

  • Open the IPTV player app you installed
  • Select Add Playlist or Add New Subscription
  • Choose URL or M3U URL option
  • Enter your M3U URL provided in your welcome email
  • Enter your EPG URL (also provided in welcome email) if prompted
  • Name your playlist (e.g., "NakedTurtle IPTV")
  • Save and enjoy your channels!

Important Note

Your M3U URL and EPG URL are unique to your account. Never share these links with others as it may result in account suspension.

Android TV Setup Guide

1

Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

First, enable installation from unknown sources:

  • Go to Settings on your Android TV
  • Select Security or Privacy
  • Enable Unknown Sources or Install unknown apps
2

Install IPTV Player

Install an IPTV player app from the Google Play Store:

  • Open the Google Play Store
  • Search for TiviMate, IPTV Smarters, or VLC
  • Select the app and click Install
3

Configure IPTV Player

Set up the IPTV player with your NakedTurtle subscription:

  • Open the IPTV player app
  • Look for Add Playlist, Add Subscription, or similar option
  • Select URL or M3U URL method
  • Enter your M3U URL from your welcome email
  • Add your EPG URL if prompted
  • Name your playlist and save
4

Optimize Playback Settings

For the best experience, adjust these settings:

  • Go to app Settings
  • Set Video Quality based on your internet speed
  • Enable Hardware Acceleration if available
  • Adjust Buffer Size if you experience any playback issues

Recommended

For Android TV boxes, we recommend using a wired ethernet connection instead of Wi-Fi for the most stable streaming experience.

Smart TV Setup Guide

1

Install IPTV Player App

Install an IPTV player app from your TV's app store:

  • For Samsung TVs: Search for Smart IPTV or SS IPTV in the Samsung App Store
  • For LG TVs: Search for Smart IPTV in the LG Content Store
  • For other Smart TVs: Look for any IPTV player app in your TV's app store
2

Configure IPTV Player

Set up the app with your NakedTurtle subscription:

  • Open the installed IPTV player app
  • Navigate to Settings or Configuration
  • Look for Add Playlist or Add M3U URL option
  • Enter your M3U URL from your welcome email
  • Add your EPG URL if prompted
3

Alternative Method: USB Loading

If your TV doesn't support direct URL input, try the USB method:

  • On a computer, open your M3U URL in a browser
  • Save the content as a file named "playlist.m3u" on a USB drive
  • Insert the USB drive into your Smart TV
  • Open your IPTV player app and look for an option to load from USB or external storage
  • Navigate to and select the playlist.m3u file
4

Optimize TV Settings

For the best streaming experience:

  • Ensure your TV is connected to a stable internet connection
  • Use a wired ethernet connection if possible
  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Adjust picture settings for optimal viewing

Compatibility Note

Some older Smart TVs may have limited processing power. If you experience performance issues, consider using an external device like a Firestick or Android TV box.

iPhone/iPad Setup Guide

1

Install IPTV Player App

Download an IPTV player app from the App Store:

  • Open the App Store on your iOS device
  • Search for one of these recommended apps:
    • IPTV Smarters Pro
    • GSE Smart IPTV
    • iPlayTV
  • Tap Get or the download icon to install
2

Configure IPTV Player

Set up the app with your NakedTurtle subscription:

  • Open the IPTV player app you installed
  • Look for Add Playlist, Add URL, or + icon
  • Select the option to add via M3U URL
  • Enter your M3U URL from your welcome email
  • Add your EPG URL if prompted
  • Name your playlist (e.g., "NakedTurtle IPTV")
  • Save the configuration
3

Adjust Playback Settings

Optimize your viewing experience:

  • Go to app Settings
  • Set Video Quality based on your internet connection
  • Adjust Buffer Size if needed
  • Enable Hardware Decoding if available
4

AirPlay to Apple TV (Optional)

To watch on a larger screen:

  • Make sure your iOS device and Apple TV are on the same Wi-Fi network
  • Start playing a channel in your IPTV app
  • Swipe down from the top-right corner (or up from the bottom on older devices) to open Control Center
  • Tap the AirPlay icon
  • Select your Apple TV from the list

Data Usage Warning

Streaming IPTV consumes significant data. We recommend using Wi-Fi instead of cellular data to avoid exceeding your mobile data plan limits.

PC/Mac Setup Guide

1

Choose a Media Player

Download and install one of these recommended media players:

  • VLC Media Player: Download here
  • Kodi: Download here
  • MyIPTV Player (Windows only): Download here
2

Configure VLC Media Player

If using VLC (the simplest option):

  • Open VLC Media Player
  • Click on Media in the top menu
  • Select Open Network Stream
  • Paste your M3U URL in the network URL field
  • Click Play
  • To save the playlist: Click Media > Save Playlist to File
3

Configure Kodi (Alternative)

If using Kodi:

  • Open Kodi
  • Go to Add-ons
  • Click the Package Icon (top-left)
  • Select Install from repository
  • Choose Kodi Add-on repository
  • Go to PVR Clients
  • Select PVR IPTV Simple Client and install it
  • After installation, go to Settings of the PVR IPTV Simple Client
  • Under General, set M3U Play List URL to your M3U URL
  • Set EPG URL to your EPG URL
  • Click OK and restart Kodi
4

Optimize Playback Settings

For the best experience on PC/Mac:

  • Ensure your computer meets the minimum requirements for HD streaming
  • Close unnecessary applications to free up system resources
  • Use a wired internet connection if possible
  • Adjust video output settings in your player for optimal performance

Pro Tip

For a more TV-like experience on your computer, consider using Kodi with the PVR IPTV Simple Client as it provides a full-featured electronic program guide and recording capabilities.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Having problems with your setup? Check these common solutions.

Channels not loading or buffering issues

Try these solutions:

  • Check your internet connection speed (minimum 10 Mbps recommended)
  • Try a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi
  • Restart your device and router
  • Increase the buffer size in your IPTV player settings
  • Try a different IPTV player app
  • Contact our support if the issue persists
EPG (TV Guide) not showing

To fix EPG issues:

  • Make sure you've entered the correct EPG URL in your player settings
  • Allow sufficient time for the EPG data to load (can take up to 5-10 minutes)
  • Try clearing the EPG cache in your player settings
  • Restart your IPTV player app
  • Check if your player supports EPG functionality
Playback errors or crashes

If your player crashes or shows errors:

  • Update your IPTV player app to the latest version
  • Check if your device has sufficient storage space
  • Try a different IPTV player app
  • Restart your device
  • For Android/Firestick: Clear app cache and data
  • Reinstall the IPTV player app
Can't find specific channels

To locate channels more easily:

  • Use the search function in your IPTV player
  • Check different categories (Sports, News, Entertainment, etc.)
  • Sort channels alphabetically if your player supports it
  • Create a favorites list for your most-watched channels
  • Contact support if you believe a channel should be available but isn't
Poor video quality

To improve video quality:

  • Check your internet speed (minimum 25 Mbps recommended for HD content)
  • Ensure no other devices are using significant bandwidth
  • Try a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi
  • Adjust video quality settings in your IPTV player
  • Enable hardware acceleration if available
  • Some channels may have inherently lower quality than others
