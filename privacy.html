6. Cookie Policy

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your device's hard drive through your web browser that enables the site's or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Type of Cookie Purpose
Session Cookies These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They enable our website to recognize you as you navigate between pages.
Persistent Cookies These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. They enable our website to remember your preferences for your next visit.
Necessary Cookies These cookies are essential for you to browse the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the site.
Preferences Cookies These cookies allow our website to remember choices you have made in the past, like what language you prefer or what your user name and password are so you can automatically log in.
Analytics Cookies These cookies collect information about how you use a website, like which pages you visited and which links you clicked on.
Marketing Cookies These cookies track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising or to limit how many times you see an ad.

6.2 Your Cookie Choices

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our website.
